Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my PIN. What do I do?
Tap the 'Forgot PIN?' link on the lock screen. You will be asked to answer the Security Question you set up in the 'Security & Recovery' section.
Can I turn off specific subjects?
Yes! Scroll up to the 'Focus Subjects' section. You can toggle off any subject (like Math or Reading) that you don't want to appear on the lock screen.
Why doesn't the app lock immediately?
The app uses a 'Lock Frequency' timer (e.g., 15 minutes) so it doesn't interrupt your child too often. You can adjust this timer in the settings above.
A question has a mistake. How do I report it?
Please take a screenshot of the question (showing the ID in the bottom corner) and email it to our support team at support@brainpass.app.
