Common Questions

Is it safe for my child?

Absolutely. BrainPass runs locally on your device. We don't sell your data or track your child's location.

What happens if they get stuck?

Our new "Story Mode" encourages reading comprehension, and we provide hints. Parents can also unlock the device anytime with a PIN.

Can I see what they are learning?

Yes! The Parent Dashboard shows you exactly which questions they answered correctly and where they might need help.
